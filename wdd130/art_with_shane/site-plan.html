Content

Home page

PURPOSE Visitors will be able to look at my art on my main page. They will see multiple artworks I have created with a title, description, and/or story by the pictures. Hovering over the image will expand it and reveal a title and clicking on the image will link the user to a separate page where there will be details about purchasing the image. The menu will have options to take the user to a seperate about me page with my life story where they can learn about me and my art experiences. This page will have a lot of images of all my artworks.

Images for the Home page

About Shane

ABOUT ME Visitors will be able to look at my art on my main page. Clicking on a link will take the user to a separate page with my life story to learn about me and my art experiences. For example: Hey everyone! My name is Shane Basham, I am 21 years old, and I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona. I love learning new languages, making art, running, cooking, listening to music, playing the piano, and playing games! Ive been making art since I was 5 years old and have taken art classes every year since. On this art portfolio I will display multiple artworks I have created with a title, description, and/or story by the pictures. I hope you enjoy and find some inspiration from my art, and I hope you find some that you really like and would love to have in your home! Check out my social media pages to see all of my art posted in order of creation! Thank you for checking out my website! Contact me here if you have any questions or have any special requests!

Images for the Page 2

Shop

PURCHASE ARTWORK There will be a link by or on every picture that will take the user to another separate page where they will be able to purchase my art. These activities are unique to my page, they will not be able to complete any of these actions and purchase my art on any other website. The visitors will need a form of payment to send me money either directly through card payment, or through venmo or PayPal when they choose the images they want to purchase.
