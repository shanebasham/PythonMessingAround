ABOUT ME Hey everyone! My name is Shane Basham, I am 21 years old, and I was born and raised in Tucson, Arizona. I love learning new languages, making art, running, cooking, listening to music, playing the piano, and playing games! Ive been making art since I was 5 years old and have taken art classes every year since. On this art portfolio I will display multiple artworks I have created with a title, description, and/or story by the pictures. I hope you enjoy and find some inspiration from my art, and I hope you find some that you really like and would love to have in your home! Check out my social media pages to see all of my art posted in order of creation! Thank you for checking out my website! Contact me here if you have any questions or have any special requests!
